About

Born and raised in South Carolina, with a degree in Parks, Recreation, and Tourism Management from Clemson University, my professional journey originally brought me to Indianapolis 15 years ago. I followed my passion to work with youth and have served within the same nonprofit organization in a variety of roles spanning my entire career.

Throughout my time in the nonprofit sector, I have been fortunate enough to lead multiple service trips, both domestic and international, in places such as Jamaica, New York City, New Orleans, Hawaii, and Chicago. My love of traveling, serving in a variety of communities, and being a part of many individuals’ personal development has really inspired me to continue my own development.

During the pandemic, I’ve come to the realization of many things but two things stand out:

1. I’m pretty good at figuring things out.

2. It's never too late to learn, grow, and aspire for more.

Through this opportunity to learn new things (i.e., Coding, Web Development, UX Design, etc.), I’m excited to see the doors and possibilities that may continue to open now that I’ve made it through the front door.
